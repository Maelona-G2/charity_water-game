Welcome to Carry the Jerry!

A game to raise awareness for Charity: water, a non-profit organization bringing clean and safe drinking water to people in developing countries.

How To Play

  • Use the arrow keys to move your character.
  • Carry the jerry cans past 3 levels to win the game.
  • Avoid obstacles along the way.
  • Reach all checkpoints to complete each level.
